Gandhi

Peace

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was the preeminent leader of the Indian independence movement in British-ruled India

Here's a time line of Mahatma Gandhi's life:

  • 1869" - born in Porbandar, a coastal town in present-day Gujarat, India,"
  • 1876" - Mohandes Gandhi Begins Primary School in Rajkot"
  • 1881" - Mohandes Gandhi Enters High School in Rajkot"
  • 1883" - Mohandes Gandhi Marries Kasturbai Makhanji in an Arranged Child Marriage"
  • 1884" - Mohandes Gandhi Enters Rebellious Adolescent Phase, Engaging in Activities He Will Soon Reject"
  • 1885" - Mohandes Gandhi's Father Dies at 63"
  • 1888" - Mohandes Gandhi Travels to London to Train as a Barrister"
  • 1891" - Mohandes Gandhi Returns to India from London After Passing the Bar"
  • 1893" - Mohandes Gandhi Travels to South Africa to Work Under a Year-Long Contract with Dada Abdulla & Co., an Indian Firm"
  • 1894" - Mohandes Gandhi Founds the Natal Indian Congress"
  • 1897" - Mohandes Gandhi is Nearly Lynched by White Settlers in Durban upon Returning from a Brief Trip to Fetch his Family in India"
  • 1899" - Gandhi Organizes an Indian Ambulance Corps of 1100 Men During His Service in the Boer War"
  • 1904" - Gandhi Founds the Phoenix Settlement"
  • 1906" - Gandhi Introduces Non-Violent Protest Philosophy of Satyagraha"
  • 1908" - Gandhi is Arrested for the First Time, for Refusing to Carry an Obligatory ID Card in South Africa"
  • 1910" - Gandhi Establishes the Tolstoy Farm, which Serves as a Base of Operations for His Activities in South Africa"
  • 1913" - Gandhi Begins "Great March" to Gain Indian Rights in South Africa"
  • 1914" - Gandhi Suspends South African Struggle After Winning Passage of the Indian Relief Act"
  • 1915" - Gandhi Receives Hero's Welcome Upon Returning to India from South Africa"
  • 1917" - Gandhi Goes to Champaran to Investigate Conditions of Local Farmers"
  • 1918" - Gandhi Begins Advocating on Behalf of Farmers in Kheda Subject to Oppressive Taxation During a Famine"
  • 1920" - The Gandhi Era of the Indian Independence Movement Begins with the Non-Cooperation Movement"
  • 1922" - Gandhi is Arrested by the British Government on Charges of Inciting Violence at Chauri Chaura"
  • 1924" - Gandhi Begins Twenty-One Day Fast in an Attempt to Reconcile Hindus and Muslims"
  • 1929" - Indian National Congress Raises the Tricolor Flag of India"
  • 1930" - Declaration of the Independence of India"
  • 1930" - Mahatma Gandhi Arrives at Dandi, Gujarat, Concluding the Salt March"
  • 1934" - Gandhi Announces Retirement from Politics to Focus on Village Economics"
  • 1939" - Gandhi Announces Fast-Unto-Death in the Effort to Form a People's Council in Rajkot"
  • 1942" - The All India Congress Committee Launches the Quit India Movement"
  • 1944" - Kasturba Gandhi Dies at Age 74"
  • 1947" - United Kingdom Passes the Indian Independence Act 1947"
  • 1948" - Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi is Assassinated by Nathuram Godse"
